Prophet Muhammad PBUH

The most influencial person

Here's a timeline of the Prophet's life

  • Birth — 570 CE: Born in Mecca into the Quraysh tribe; later known in Mecca for honesty and trustworthiness.
  • Early Orphanhood — ~576 CE: Lost his father before birth and his mother Amina when he was a child; raised by his grandfather and then his uncle Abu Talib.
  • Youth & Reputation — late 6th century CE: Worked as a shepherd and trader; earned the nickname Al-Amin (the Trustworthy).
  • First Revelation — 610 CE: Received the first revelation from the angel Gabriel; this marks the beginning of his prophethood.
  • Early Preaching in Mecca — 610–622 CE: Began calling people to monotheism; faced growing opposition and persecution from many Meccan leaders.
  • The Night Journey (Isra) and Ascension (Mi'raj) — tradition: ~621 CE: A significant spiritual event in Islamic tradition in which he was taken from Mecca to Jerusalem and ascended to the heavens.
  • Farewell Pilgrimage & Final Sermon — 632 CE (10 AH): Performed the pilgrimage to Mecca, delivered a farewell address emphasizing rights, equality, and guidance for the community.
